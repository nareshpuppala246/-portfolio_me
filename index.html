About Me

I'm Naresh Puppala, a Firmware Engineer with a strong focus on embedded systems where hardware and software work together. I enjoy building reliable and efficient firmware for microcontrollers and IoT devices, especially those that need to run on low power.

With a strong foundation in C, C++, Python, and real-time systems such as FreeRTOS, combined with expertise in PCB design and hardware validation, I bring end-to-end insight into the product development lifecycle—from initial design through to deployment.

My work includes designing robust firmware, debugging hardware issues, and making sure everything works smoothly together. I’ve worked on projects like a large-scale Smart Payphone System and a Smart Mailbox with sensors, where I aim to deliver smart, connected, and dependable solutions

Projects

Smart Payphone System (RFID + GSM)

Designed a prepaid GSM payphone system for remote areas. Users authenticate with RFID cards, and upon tapping, the system verifies identity, checks the balance, and manages call permissions. Charges are calculated in real time and deducted from the card balance, enabling secure communication without traditional landlines.

Tech Stack:
  • STM32, C/C++
  • RFID Module, GSM (EC200CN)
  • MongoDB

Tech Stack:

  • AI Optimization with ML Model

Smart Farming & Crop Monitoring
